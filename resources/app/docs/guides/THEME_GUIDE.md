### Method 1: Settings Page
1. Open the app
2. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
3. Look for **"Appearance Theme"** section at the top
4. Select from dropdown:
   - üåü Glassmorphism (Modern)
   - ‚öôÔ∏è Steampunk (Victorian)

In Settings, click on the theme preview cards to instantly switch
